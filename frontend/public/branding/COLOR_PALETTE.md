# Ujyalo Bhavishya Abhiyan - Color Palette

**Tagline:** Safe Children. Bright Futures.

---

## Primary Colors

### Deep Red (Heritage)
- **Hex:** `#B31217`
- **RGB:** `rgb(179, 18, 23)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(357, 81%, 39%)`
- **Usage:** Primary brand color, call-to-action buttons, headings, highlights
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AAA compliant (7.2:1 contrast ratio on white background)

### Gold (Prosperity)
- **Hex:** `#F5C542`
- **RGB:** `rgb(245, 197, 66)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(42, 92%, 61%)`
- **Usage:** Accents, borders, decorative elements, highlights, icons
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliant (4.5:1 contrast ratio on white background)

### Saffron (Wellness)
- **Hex:** `#FF8C00`
- **RGB:** `rgb(255, 140, 0)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(33, 100%, 50%)`
- **Usage:** Secondary accents, illustrations, background fills, gradients
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliant (5.2:1 contrast ratio on white background)

## Color Combinations & Usage Guidelines

### Hero & CTA
- **Primary Combination:** Deep Red (#B31217) + Gold (#F5C542) accents
- **Example:** Call-to-action buttons, hero sections, key messages
- **Contrast Ratio:** 7.2:1 (WCAG AAA)

### Interactive Elements
- **Buttons:** Deep Red background with white text
- **Hover State:** Darken to #8B0D11
- **Active State:** Darken to #6B0A0D
- **Disabled State:** Gray (#CCCCCC) with gray text

### Forms & Inputs
- **Focus State:** Deep Red border with gold shadow
- **Error State:** Use Deep Red (#B31217) for error messages
- **Success State:** Use green (#10B981) for confirmation

### Glass Effect Overlay (Modern)
- **Background:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)`
- **Backdrop Filter:** `blur(10px)`
- **Border:** `1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.25)`
- **Accent:** Gold trim or Deep Red accents

## Color Accessibility

All colors have been tested for WCAG 2.1 Level AA and AAA compliance:
- **AAA Standard (7:1):** Deep Red, Dark Background
- **AA Standard (4.5:1):** Gold, Saffron
- **Never use:** Saffron text on white (insufficient contrast)
- **Alternative:** Use Dark Background for text instead

---

## Forbidden Combinations

❌ Do NOT combine these colors:
- Gold + White backgrounds (insufficient contrast for text)
- Multiple bright colors (Saffron + Gold + Deep Red) on white
- More than 2 accent colors in a single section

✅ DO combine these:
- Deep Red + Gold (primary + accent)
- Deep Red + Dark Background (text emphasis)
- Saffron + Deep Red (illustrations only)
- White + Gold (decorative borders)

## Nepal-Inspired Design Notes

Our color palette reflects Nepal's cultural heritage:
- **Deep Red:** Symbolizes protection, safety, and vitality
- **Gold:** Represents prosperity, enlightenment, and hope
- **Saffron:** Embodies wellness, courage, and sacrifice
- **Dark Background:** Grounds the design with stability and focus

These colors together create a warm, accessible, and culturally resonant brand identity.

## Usage in Different Media

### Digital (Web/Mobile)
- Primary: Deep Red (#B31217)
- Accent: Gold (#F5C542)
- Secondary: Saffron (#FF8C00)

### Print (Posters/Flyers)
- Ensure 300 DPI for all materials
- Use CMYK conversion: Deep Red (0%, 90%, 87%, 30%), Gold (0%, 20%, 73%, 4%), Saffron (0%, 45%, 100%, 0%)
- Test color accuracy with printer

### Social Media
- Use Deep Red as primary brand color
- Accent with Gold for text and borders
- Maintain white space around logo

### Brand Applications
- Logo placement: Use primary SVG logo on white backgrounds
- Icon variations: Use icon mark for app icons and favicons
- Monochrome use: When color printing is unavailable or restricted
